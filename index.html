what does it do?

this RPG is a shoulder-fired anti-tank weapon that uses rocket propulsion to launch a grenade towards its target. The primary purpose of an RPG is to engage and destroy armored vehicles, such as tanks or armored personnel carriers. When a rocket-propelled grenade is fired, it ignites a propellant charge to propel the grenade out of the launcher, and then the rocket motor takes over to accelerate it towards the target. The rocket motor provides the necessary thrust for the grenade to maintain its velocity and trajectory. Additionally, RPGs are also used by infantry units to engage fortified positions, bunkers, or buildings. The explosive warhead of the RPG is designed to penetrate the armor of a target, either by direct impact or by detonating nearby and causing spalling (shards of metal) to penetrate the target's hull. This makes the RPG a versatile weapon against a range of armored vehicles.
